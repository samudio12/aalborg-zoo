CHRISTMAS

×
Aalborg Zoo is a proud supporter of the BLACK MAMBA project which consists of a group of 26 local women operating in Balule (South Africa) where they protect rhinos and elephants against poachers.

As a matter of fact, the trade in endangered species today is considered the world's fourth largest criminal economy. In less than 2 years, “Black Mamba” has reduced the poaching in those areas by 76%. During the same period, they found 12 camps and 3 slaughterhouses, all made of poachers. There were averaging 100 elephants a day.

If the poaching continues at the present pace, there will be no elephants left in Africa in 10 years. Indeed, there are under 25,000 rhinos in Africa and 83% of all African rhinos live in South Africa.

Show that you care

Join us and contribute for a good thing

Although there are many people who love animals and enjoy spending time around them, only a few of these actually do something in order to protect them.

This year at Aalborg Zoo you have the opportunity to save hundreds of elephants and rhinos by supporting Aalborg Zoo Conservation Foundation (AZCF).

Even a small donation can make a lasting difference since little steps can lead to significant results and have a great impact on the protection of wildlife.

Unfortunately, the number of endangered species grow every year due to the greedy cruelty of merciless snipers.

We believe that the education of the community can raise public awareness and stop this terrible haunt.

Aalborg Zoo pays a huge attention to vulnerable and endangered species in order to save them from extinction by taking action around the affected areas such as in South Africa where volunteers fight against poachers of ivory and rhinos.

Thanks to these actions, elephants and rhinos are no longer endangered according to IUCN Red List African. However, they are still vulnerable which remains a serious issue.

The illegal hunting of the wild animals in Africa has almost exploded through the last decade especially elephants and rhinos.

Therefore, we would like to encourage every soul to take one step against extinction by joining us on Christmas Eve at Aalborg Zoo!

Christmas
Event

The best Chrimstas Eve begins in the Zoo with Christmas music and worship. Come and join us when our Christmas eve event this year has a 10th anniversary. Meet Santa, have plenty of fun, have a happy Christmas party for everyone, and make sure to greet the reindeer before going on gift delivery. If you still have a single Christmas gift or this year's gift, the ZooShop is open and can offer a lot of exciting gift ideas. You are also very welcome to give a Christmas present to Africa's wild elephants if you support our collection for the Black Mamba anti-schoot project in South Africa.
